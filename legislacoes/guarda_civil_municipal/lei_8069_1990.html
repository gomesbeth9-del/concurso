The content of the provided URL, "https://www.planalto.gov.br/ccivil_03/leis/l8069.htm", is the "LEI Nº 8.069, DE 13 DE JULHO DE 1990", also known as the Estatuto da Criança e do Adolescente (Child and Adolescent Statute) from Brazil. [1]The document outlines the integral protection of children and adolescents, defining a child as up to twelve years of age incomplete, and an adolescent as between twelve and eighteen years of age. I[1]t covers fundamental rights, including the right to life, health, liberty, respect, dignity, and family and community coexistence. T[1]he law also addresses aspects like adoption, guardianship, and the duties of parents, the community, society, and public authorities in ensuring these rights. I[1]t includes various amendments and additions over the years, with some provisions having future effective dates.[1] Sources: [1] L8069 - Planalto (https://www.planalto.gov.br/ccivil_03/leis/l8069.htm)
